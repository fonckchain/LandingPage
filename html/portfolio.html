    GameStop - $GME | How to Direct Register with Computershare
    1. Open an account with an international broker that is able to transfer stocks, such as Interactive Brokers (IBKR).
    2. Purchase $GME on the broker's account.
    3. Transfer the stocks out of the brokerage to the official transfer agent of GameStop, Computershare.
    4. Computershare will create an account for you, and you'll need to call them for a verification.
    5. Your stocks are now held under your name and can't be used for liquidity by the corrupted system.
